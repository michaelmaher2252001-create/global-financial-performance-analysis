# 📊 Global Financial Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents a **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze global financial performance with a focus on **sales, cost of goods sold (COGS), and profitability**.  
The dashboard is built to support **management decision-making** by transforming raw financial data into clear, actionable insights.

---

## 🏢 Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to gain a consolidated view of financial performance across regions and product lines.  
Without clear visibility, it becomes difficult to:
- Identify profitable and unprofitable areas
- Control costs effectively
- Respond early to performance risks

This project addresses these challenges by providing an executive-level financial overview.

---

## 🔍 Key Questions Answered
- How are global sales and profits performing overall?
- Which regions contribute most to profitability?
- Where are cost pressures impacting margins?
- What trends can be observed over time?

---

## 📈 Key Metrics & KPIs
- Total Sales
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
- Gross Profit
- Profit Trends
- Regional Performance Indicators

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Executive KPI cards for quick financial assessment
- Sales and profit breakdown by region
- Trend analysis over time
- Clear, interactive visualizations for business users

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- A small number of regions generate the highest share of profit
- Some high-sales regions show lower margins due to cost pressure
- Clear trends help identify periods of growth and decline

---

## ✅ Business Value
This analysis helps decision‑makers to:
- Focus on high‑margin regions
- Identify cost optimization opportunities
- Improve strategic financial planning
- Support data‑driven management discussions
